## WB RACE STRATEGY (locked, evidence-based)
- **Even sub-sets + fixed micro-rest beats large-set-then-fragment.** Detonating at rep 12–15 spike = long recovery = net slower.
- Race plan: **6×15 + 1×10 (=100), ~7s rest, exhale every rep, OPEN PALM (no clench → protects forearm).**
- Fresh ~4:30; race-day fatigued (last station) realistically ~5:30–6:30 + clean rep standard.
- Cadence ~2.2s/rep. Limiter = breathing/CO2 tolerance + composure, NOT legs/engine.
